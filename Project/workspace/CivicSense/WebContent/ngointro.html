The Non governmental organisations are an integral part of the society. NGOs are not for private personal profit or gain.The aims of NGOs are to improve the circumstances and prospects of people and to act on concerns and issues detrimental to the well-being, circumstances, or prospects of people or society as a whole.


Our system includes these members of the society also striving towards betterment of the community.NGOs can also register to our systems and login to their accounts.We provide them with a facility to create groups and ensure that their cause is put forth amidst the public and the authorities to bring it to their notice.People can extend their support to the cause of the NGO's
