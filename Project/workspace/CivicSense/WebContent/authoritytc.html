Municipal authorities are provided with their own accounts which ensures that
citizens are in direct communication with the authorities.Municipal authorities
need to contact the administrator to receive the username and password.

We allow authorities to filter invalid complaints by marking them as inappropriate.
Authorities can also change the status of the complaint to make their response transparent
to the users.We provide a system wherein the authorities find it easy and convenient to
respond to the complaints of the citizens.

This not only ensures the effectiveness of the grievance system but also provides the authorities
with a sense of duty and satisfaction of serving the citizens in a direct manner.



Tips for our municipal authorities

  • A memo will be received from the administrator for the faulty response.
  • Credits for the authority will be reduced accordingly
  • Authorities need to change the status as and when progress is made
